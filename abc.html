Dashboard Overview

On our main dashboard, you will find a wealth of information. Below, we present the features and actions available to you, organized step by step for enhanced clarity.

1. Navbar Options

Collapse Sidebar

A button with three horizontal lines allows you to collapse or expand the sidebar, thereby improving your visibility of the dashboard.

Steps to collapse sidebar:

  1. Please click on the button with three lines.
  2. After clicking that button, you will notice that the sidebar has collapsed.

Add Functionality

A plus icon button allows you to add various items, including the ability to add a community, collection, elevation, user role, and user.

Steps to add functionality:

  1. Begin by clicking on the + icon.
  2. You can now observe the added functionality for various elements, including community, collection, elevation, user roles, and the ability to add users.

Search Functionality

A sidebar aids in searching design elements, enhancing user experience.

To implement the search functionality, follow these steps:

  1. To begin your search, simply click on the search box to enter your query.
  2. Type your search query into the search box.

Activity

This section is dedicated to Activity, enabling you to view all activity related to your account.

Steps to check your activity log:

  1. Click on the book icon to access your recent activity.
  2. You can now view your recent activity.

Notifications

This section is dedicated to notifications, enabling you to view all updates related to your account.

Steps to view notifications:

  1. Click on the notification icon.
  2. You can now view notifications.

Settings

The settings button allows you to update your personal information and subscription plan, as well as explore alternative plans.

Steps to update details:

  1. To access the user details, click on the settings button, which will open the settings menu.
  2. You can update your information and save the changes here.

Logout Functionality:

In this section, you have the option to log out of the visualizer.

Follow these steps to successfully log out:

  1. Click on the profile icon.
  2. Click the logout button to sign out of your account.
