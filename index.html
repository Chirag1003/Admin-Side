Admin Side Documentation

Wizard

Wizard feature includes creating elevation, collection, community, and updating the blueprint.

Access the Wizard Section

From the left-hand sidebar of the admin panel, click on the "Wizard" menu option.

This will open the Wizard Dashboard, where you can manage different components of the ULE 360 experience.

Create a New Option

On the "Create Options" page, click the purple "+" button (highlighted in the image) to add a new option.

Delete Created Options

Click the delete icon to remove a single option.

Edit Mode

Locate the "Edit Mode" switch on the top right of the options panel and Click the switch to enable editing.

Import Existing Options

Instead of creating new options manually, you can quickly reuse previously created ones using the Import Existing feature.

Locate the "Import Existing" button at the top right.

Select Items to Import

After clicking on Import Existing, a page will open with a list of reusable options.

  1. View Available Items:
    • A table is displayed showing titles and statuses of all previously created items (e.g., Brick, Door, Fascia).
    • Each item is marked Active, indicating it's ready to be imported.
  2. Select the Items You Need:
    • Use the checkboxes on the left to select individual items.
    • To select all items, click the "All" checkbox at the top.

Click "Save Changes" for changes to be applied.

In the Search bar we search the wizard by the title

Managing Listings

  1. Click on "Manage Listing" on the left-handsidebar.
  2. Now click on the "Communities" button

Communities Section

In this feature you can manage community section

The Community section displays individual community cards showing the Community Name (e.g., "Arbor Spring," "Fairhope"), along with edit options and an Active toggle to mark communities as Active or Inactive. At the top right of the dashboard, action buttons allow for Bulk Edit to modify multiple entries, Upload to import details from external files, Undo to revert recent changes, and Delete to remove selected entries.

Steps to manage communities:

  1. Click on the "Upload" button
  2. Now, Upload the CSV file and click on the "Continue" button.
  3. Now Click on the "+ icon" on the top right corner for adding the new community.
  4. Fill all the required fields and then click on "Add Community" button.
  5. From here user can directly edit the collection and delete the selected collection.

Collection Section

In this feature you can search for specific collection, edit, delete, link collection with community.

  • Click on the "Collection" button highlighted with a red box.
  • Click on "Link Collection to Community" if you want to link collection.
  • Click on the "Deleted" button for deleting the collection.

Elevation Section

The "Elevations" option is designed for managing elevation designs or property elevation collections. By clicking this option, users can view the current listings of elevation assets, and likely perform actions such as adding new elevations, editing existing designs, or removing listings.

  • Click on the "Elevations" button under Manage Listing to access elevations.
  • Click on "Blueprint" button to upload elevation blueprint.
  • Upload your blueprint from your directory and click on "Upload Blueprint" button.
  • Click here to search specific elevation.
  • From here you can view linked collection list and edit or delete elevation.

Options

The purpose of this section is to provide detailed management tools for various options. Users can toggle the Status to activate or deactivate each option, and use the Edit or Delete buttons to make necessary modifications.

  1. Click on the "Options" button on the left sidebar.

Search Options

From here you can search for specific options.

Create New Options

  1. Click on the + button to create a new option.
  2. Now, to add a new option, fill in the Title field with the name of the option, select "Yes" or "No" to set its activation status, and click Add Option to save the entry.

Add Option Layer

  1. Click on the + button to add option layer.
  2. Now select layers from the dropdown.
  3. After selecting the layer click on "Save Changes".

Change Option Status

Click on the green toggle button to change the status.

Edit Option

Click on the edit icon under Action to edit the Option.

Delete Option

Click on the trash icon under Action to delete the option.

Option Edit Mode

  1. Click on the Edit Mode toggle near profile icon to open Edit Mode.
  2. Now make changes as needed and click on save changes.

Undo Changes

Click on Undo button near delete to undo previous changes.

Leads

This section is used to manage leads. Here you can view details like the Buyer's name, the associated Community, specific Collection and Elevation details, and the current Status of the lead.

  • Click on "Leads" button on the left sidebar.

    • Search and Change Lead Status

    1. Click on "Search" button to search for buyers using their ID number or name.
    2. Select the desired entries via checkboxes, then click "Change Status" to update their current status.
    3. Now select the status as desired.
    4. After selecting the desired status click on "Save Changes" .

    User Management

    In this feature you can update status, add new entry, modify names, descriptions, or statuses, manage permissions and also remove them when necessary.

    User Roles

    In User Roles you can update the status of roles (active/inactive), add new roles, modify role names, descriptions, or statuses, and update role permissions.

    1. Click on "User Roles" under "User Management" on the left sidebar to access the User Roles.

    Add New Role

    1. Click on the "+" Icon on the top right corner.
    2. Enter the details of the role, including the name, description, and status (active or inactive). Then, click on the submit button.

    Modify Any Role

    1. Click on the edit icon in front of the role you want to edit.
    2. Update the details you want to change, , then click on the submit button.

    Update Role Permissions

    1. Click on the three horizontal dots below the action column.
    2. Update the values to "Yes" or "No" from the dropdown to specify whether the user can or cannot access that feature.

    User Management

    Add New User

    1. Click on the "+" Icon
    2. Enter the details of the User, including the name, email, password, mobile number, role and status (active or inactive). Then, click on the Create User button.

    Modify Any User

    1. Click on the edit icon in front of the user you want to edit.
    2. Update the details you want to change, , then click on the submit button.

    Delete Any User

    1. Click on the delete icon in front of the user you want to delete.

    Billing and Payment

    In this section, you can view your billing details and subscription information.

    1. Click on Billing & Payments in left sidebar to view your billing details and subscription information.
    2. Click on Re-send Invoice button to resend your invoice.
